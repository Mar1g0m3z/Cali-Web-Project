Welcome to San Francisco, California!
Established in 1850

Region:
San Francisco is located in Northern California, on the tip of a peninsula, surrounded by the Pacific Ocean and San Francisco Bay.

Classification: San Francisco is classified as an urban city, being a densely populated major metropolitan area.

Population:
Approximately 815,201

Average Income Level:
The median household income in San Francisco is around $126,000, which is significantly higher than the state median income of California, reflecting a much higher average income level than the state average.
