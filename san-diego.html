Welcome to San Diego California!
Established in 1850

Region:
San Diego is located in Southern California, near the U.S.-Mexico border along the Pacific Coast.

Classification:
San Diego is classified as an urban city with some suburban areas, being one of the largest cities in California and a major economic hub.

Population:
Approximately 1.39 million

Average Income Level:
The median household income in San Diego is around $89,000, which is slightly higher than the state median income of California (around $84,000), indicating a relatively higher income level compared to the state average.
